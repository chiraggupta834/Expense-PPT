Current Challenges

Problem Statement

Manual Tracking

Reliance on paper trails, spreadsheets, and emails creates a fragmented and error-prone process that scales poorly.

Errors and Delays

Manual data entry leads to calculation mistakes and slower cycles.

No Proper Approval Flow

Unstructured email chains result in lost requests and unclear authority.

Lack of Visibility

Users cannot see where claims are stuck in the process.

04
Access Control

User Roles & Access

Standard

Employee

Standard access for all staff members.

  • Submit Vouchers: Create new claims with receipts attached.
  • Track Status: View real-time status updates (Pending, Approved).
  • Edit Claims: Modify and resubmit rejected vouchers.
Manager

Manager

Supervisory access for team oversight.

  • Review Vouchers: Detailed inspection of team claims.
  • Decision Making: Approve valid claims or reject non-compliant.
  • Add Remarks: Provide feedback or request clarification.
Admin

Administrator

Full system control and processing.

  • Final Approval: Secondary validation and payment authorize.
  • Reporting: Access financial reports and analytics.
  • System Monitoring: Audit logs and user management.
